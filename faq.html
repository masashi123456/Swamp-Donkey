Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?
Swamp Donkey Mobile Mechanic primarily serves the Rexburg, Idaho area and surrounding communities within a 30-mile radius. If you are outside this area, please call us, as we may still be able to assist you for an additional travel fee.
What are your operating hours?
Our standard hours are Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays. Emergency roadside assistance may be available outside these hours; please call for availability.
Do I need to provide parts or tools?
No, you do not. Swamp Donkey comes fully equipped with the tools necessary for most repairs. We also source high-quality parts for your vehicle. If you have already purchased your own parts, we can install them, but please note that we cannot warranty parts provided by the customer.
How does mobile mechanic pricing compare to a traditional shop?
Our pricing is often more competitive than traditional brick-and-mortar shops because we have lower overhead costs. We provide transparent quotes before starting any work. You get the convenience of us coming to you without the premium price tag.
What types of vehicles do you service?
We service most makes and models of cars, light trucks, and SUVs. We currently do not service heavy-duty diesel trucks, RVs, or European specialty vehicles. If you have a question about your specific vehicle, please contact us.
What if I don't know what's wrong with my car?
That's not a problem. We offer diagnostic services. Our mechanic will come to your location, inspect the vehicle, and use diagnostic equipment to determine the issue. We charge a standard diagnostic fee, which is often waived or rolled into the cost of the repair if you choose us to fix the problem.
How do I schedule an appointment?
You can schedule an appointment by calling us at 208-547-8375, emailing us, or using the Quick Quote form on our website.
